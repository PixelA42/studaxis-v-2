Algebra — Chapter 1

This is a short sample textbook content for testing the RAG implementation.

Section 1: Basics
Algebra studies symbols and the rules for manipulating those symbols. In elementary algebra, those symbols represent numbers without fixed values, known as variables.

Section 2: Equations
An equation states that two expressions are equal. Solving equations involves finding values for variables that make the equation true.

Section 3: Practice Problems
